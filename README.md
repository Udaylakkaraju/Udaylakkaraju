### Featured Projects

**[Supply Chain SLA Risk & Profit Protection](https://github.com/Udaylakkaraju/supply-chain-risk-analysis)**
57.3% of 65,752 orders miss their delivery promise. Found that high-value orders breach at the same rate as low-value ones ($2.31M exposed) and that 5 of dozens of shipping lanes drive 41.2% of all breaches. BigQuery SQL → Power BI → Excel, three pilot-validated fixes quantified.

**[Streaming Content Investment & Franchise Risk](https://github.com/Udaylakkaraju/Cine-Roi)**
Built a custom Audience Value Index across 4,562 films and statistically validated it before using it. Horror returns 11.4 audience-value points per $1M budget — more than double any other genre — while franchise sequels fall to 61% of the opener's value by film 2.

**[Pediatric CHD Care Pathway Analytics](https://github.com/Udaylakkaraju/pediatric-chd-pathway-analytics)**
Modeled a 15,000-patient diagnostic pathway across five care stages on a 100K-patient synthetic EHR dataset. Only 26.3% of symptomatic patients ever reach diagnosis; two targeted fixes to the biggest drop-off points model +610 recovered diagnoses.

**[Payment Failure & Fraud Review Analytics](https://github.com/Udaylakkaraju/Payment-Fraud-Detection)**
51,237 synthetic transactions, 12.92% failure rate. Two decline reasons account for 85% of $500K in failed value, and a capacity-limited fraud review queue reaches 9.5% precision vs. a 5.5% random baseline.
